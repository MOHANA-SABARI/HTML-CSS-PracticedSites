RESPONSIVE CARDS

Twitter

Twitter, founded in 2006 by Jack Dorsey, Noah Glass, Biz Stone, and Evan Williams, is a social media platform known for its microblogging format. Users post "tweets"—short messages limited initially to 140 characters, later expanded to 280 characters in 2017. These tweets can include text, photos, videos, and links.
